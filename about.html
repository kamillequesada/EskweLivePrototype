EskweLive – Thrive with Eskwelive

Make the classroom instantly accessible with just one download. Watch live videos about your favorite subjects, learn from educators all over the world, or the world could learn from you! Connect with other students and educators through a real time live chat.

Eskwelive can do that and more, with features such as private channels for schools and public channels for unaffiliated teachers, as well as quizzes that pop up from the live stream. Students can also view PowerPoints and Lesson documents needed.

01

Create an account

A single username and password gets you into Eswelive. Setup your profile, view your subscriptions, chat with Eswelivers, and many more.

02

Choose an EskweLiver

You can subscribe to an Esweliver you like to learn from. Once you subscribe, any new videos and livestreams they published, whether free or paid will show up in your Subscriptions feed.

03

Enjoy EskweLive!

Learn and connect with educators and students around the world. Explore the different categories we have in offer, and learn much more than that.
